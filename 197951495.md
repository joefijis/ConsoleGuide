1. Click on the *Approve* *Interstate Date Sharing for Health Entities* button, located on the left-hand side of the screen.

2. Select a PDMP site from the list of PDMP sites, located on the left side of the screen.

QQ has been selected for demonstration purposes.

1. Review the list of HCE’s associated with the selected site, as displayed on the right-side of the screen.

2. Click on a healthcare entity to view expanded details relating to its site configuration and facilities data.

3. Grant or revoke data-sharing access to a healthcare entity by:

   1. Pressing the orange *Grant Access* button to enable interstate data-sharing for that site, or
   2. Pressing the blue *Deny Access* button to disable interstate data-sharing access for that site.

4. Verify that your action has been successfully implemented by looking for the following alerts:

   1. If access was granted, an alert that states “Site Unblocked Successfully”, or
   2. If access was revoked, an alert that states “Site Blocked Successfully”
