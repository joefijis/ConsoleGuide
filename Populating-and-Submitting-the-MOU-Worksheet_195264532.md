1. Click on the *Interstate Data Sharing* button, followed by the *MOU Worksheet(s)* option, located on the left-hand side of the screen.

2. The *MOU Worksheet(s)* page is displayed.

3. Click on the *Fill New Worksheet* button (if you have not completed a worksheet yet) or *View Saved Worksheet* (if you have previously completed a worksheet).

4. On the new *MOU Worksheet* page, populate the following sections:

* State Administration’s Information
* User Validation Procedures
* Data Access and Security
* Data Elements for Request Submissions
* Query Response
* Miscellaneous

Fields with a red asterisk ( \* ) are required.

1. Click the *Save* button to confirm and record your changes to the MOU worksheet.

2. Alternatively, you can press the *Cancel* button to discard the changes and return to the previous screen.

3. After saving, the *View Saved Worksheet* button will be displayed.

4. Click on *View Saved Worksheet* button to view the saved MOU Worksheet. PDMP Administrators can update the contents and/or share the MOU Worksheet with other state(s).

5. To edit the worksheet, click on the *Update* button.

6. To share the worksheet with another state, click on the *Share* button.

7. Select a state from the dropdown menu and click the *Share Form* button to send the form to the selected state.
