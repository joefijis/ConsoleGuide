nav_order: 81
---
1. Click on either the *Incoming* or *Outgoing* button located below the filters on the RxCheck Dashboard screen. This will allow you to view the selected requests.

The *Incoming* option is selected and displayed by default.

1. Perform the steps from the “Apply filters to the RxCheck Dashboard” section of this guide.

2. Select the *View by Status* button to view a graphical representation of the data, based on the status.

3. Select the *View by Role* button to view the total count of requests made by professionals in a certain role.

The steps are the same between incoming and outgoing requests.
