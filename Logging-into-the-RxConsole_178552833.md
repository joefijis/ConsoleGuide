RxCheck operates two distinct environments: **Production** and **Test (UAT)**.
Access to each environment is determined by the specific URL used. The URL will indicate if you are connected to the test or production site and the login page displays the current application version at the bottom right of the page.

**Production Link:** <https://console.rxcheck.org/rxconsole/#/login>

**UAT Link:** <https://test.rxcheck.org:18803/tetrus-rxcheck/#/login>

---

1. Navigate to the desired RxCheck environment in a web browser to log in.

***Note:*** At the bottom of the page, there is text indicating the site type is production“(PROD)”, along with the RxConsole version number.

![image-20251218-154040.png](attachments/178552833/178978817.png?width=187)

Personal login credentials are required to log into the RxConsole application. Login credentials will be provided to the state PDMP administrators during the onboarding process. **For further inquiries regarding your login credentials, please contact the RxCheck help desk**.

1. Enter your RxCheck username in the first text box. Enter your RxCheck Password in the second text box. Click on the *Sign In* button.
2. Users can receive the one time password (OTP) two ways:

a. Click *Email OTP Code* on the login screen to have the code sent to their login email.

b. Use one of the three approved authenticator apps to generate the OTP on their mobile device.
